## Getting Started <a name="getting-started"></a>

### Installation
StartLink is available for both Android and iOS devices. You can also access it via our web platform.
1. Download the app from your respective App Store, or visit the StartLink Web Portal.
2. Sign up using your email and a secure password.

### Onboarding
Upon first login, the app will ask you to choose your **Primary Role** (Innovator, Investor, or Mentor) and fill out a quick onboarding questionnaire to tailor your experience.

## User Roles & Profiles <a name="user-roles--profiles"></a>

StartLink supports three distinct user journeys. Your profile acts as your business card on the platform.

- **Innovators (Founders/Creators):** Build a profile highlighting your skills, past projects, and the core ideas you are bringing to life.
- **Investors (Angels/VCs):** Showcase your investment thesis, portfolio, and the domains you are most interested in funding.
- **Mentors (Advisors/Experts):** Highlight your industry experience, areas of specialization, and availability to help budding startups.

*You can switch roles or add a secondary role in the Settings menu under **Role Management**.*

## For Innovators: Building Your Vision <a name="for-innovators"></a>

As an Innovator, StartLink provides a suite of tools to help you ideate, build, and fund your startup.

- **Idea Management (Idea DNA):** Use the unified "Idea DNA" framework to outline your startup's core proposition, market size, and business model.
- **Pitch Health:** Upload your pitch deck and receive automated analysis on its strength, clarity, and readiness for investors.
- **Networking & Matching:** Use the Smart Matching engine to find Co-founders, team members, or Mentors who complement your skill gaps.
- **Applications:** Apply to join existing projects or accept requests from others looking to join your venture.

---

## For Investors: Finding the Next Unicorn <a name="for-investors"></a>

Investors get access to curated deal flows and deep AI-driven insights to make informed decisions.

- **Pitch Requests:** A streamlined inbox for incoming pitches. You can filter them by industry, capital required, and "Aura" score.
- **AI Insights:** For every startup you view, the StartLink AI breaks down the pros, cons, and competitive landscape.
- **Design Showcase:** Browse product designs, prototypes, and MVPs visually before committing to introductory calls.
- **Due Diligence (Verification):** Know exactly who you're dealing with. Look for the "Verified" badge on founder profiles, backed by our robust trust system.

---

## For Mentors: Guiding the Future <a name="for-mentors"></a>

Give back to the ecosystem by sharing your expertise.

- **Mentor Dashboard:** Organize your advisory sessions, track the startups you are currently mentoring, and view incoming requests.
- **Achievements:** Earn badges and "Aura" points for continuous contribution to the ecosystem, visible on your public profile.
- **Collaborative Feedback:** Leave structured, actionable feedback on ideas directly within the innovator's dashboard.

## Core Platform Features <a name="core-platform-features"></a>

### AI Co-Founder & Feedback <a name="ai-features"></a>
Feel stuck? StartLink includes a state-of-the-art **AI Co-Founder**. 
- Discuss product roadblocks, practice your investor pitch, or ask for go-to-market strategies.
- The **AI Feedback loop** automatically flags incomplete sections of your startup profile to ensure you always put your best foot forward.

### StartLink Aura & Trust System <a name="trust-system"></a>
Trust is everything in the startup ecosystem. 
- **StartLink Aura:** A comprehensive reputation score based on peer reviews, successful investments, responsiveness, and profile completeness. 
- A high Aura score dramatically increases your visibility across the platform.

### Messaging & Collaboration <a name="collaboration"></a>
- **Real-time Chat:** Seamlessly communicate with potential investors, co-founders, or mentors.
- **Messaging Dashboard:** Keep your conversations organized by dealing efficiently with pitch queries or scheduling meetings.

## Account Management & Security <a name="account-management"></a>

Your data is secured with enterprise-grade encryption.
- **Privacy Controls:** You decide who can view your Ideas. Set your startup to "Stealth Mode" if you only want it visible to verified investors.
- **Support & Debugging:** Encounter a bug? Shake your phone to access the **Debug Menu** or submit a ticket directly from the app.
